Web Installation

Quick Install Demo

Step 1

Create an account at https://koding.com, check your email, and verify your new account by setting up a password.
Go back to Koding, and then click on the button at the top.

Step 2

Type or copy-paste, and run each one of the following commands:

Downloading
wget "webULAFFpkg.tar.bz2"

Extracting (should take about 30 seconds)
tar -jxf webULAFFpkg.tar.bz2

Running
ULAFF/LAFF

Step 3

Replace "username" in http://username.kd.io:8888 with your own username.

Once you go to that address and get the warning message, just go ahead and
"Click to continue: username.kd.io:8888"
